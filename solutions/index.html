03

N₂O & F-Gas Targeting — Not CO₂

While most solutions address CO₂ — the lowest-GWP greenhouse gas — GreenAiriva targets nitrous oxide (N₂O) and fluorinated gases. N₂O carries approximately 273 times the warming impact of CO₂ per kilogram (IPCC AR6); F-gases reach several thousand times. Every captured kilogram of these gases delivers dramatically more CO₂e reduction — reflected directly in your compliance reports.

Result: outsized CO₂e impact per unit — driven by targeting what matters most, not what's most abundant.

04

Grid-Independent Autonomy

Deploys wherever emissions are highest — regardless of local power infrastructure. High-emission corridors, underserved zones, and remote facility perimeters are all accessible without requiring grid connection or civil works.

Result: no infrastructure barrier between you and the deployment.

Verifiability

Not an Estimate. A Documented Result.

GreenAiriva does not report projected or modelled reductions. Every unit produces specific, mass-based reduction data — figures your compliance team, your auditor, and your regulator can review, challenge, and act on.

This matters because the regulatory landscape is hardening. TSRS-ESG, CBAM, and municipal climate plan frameworks are beginning to distinguish between modelled estimates and auditable evidence. GreenAiriva data is designed for that distinction — from day one of deployment, not retrofitted later.
