03

N₂O & F-Gas Targeting — Not CO₂

While most solutions target CO₂ — the lowest-GWP greenhouse gas — GreenAiriva targets nitrous oxide (N₂O) and fluorinated gases. N₂O carries approximately 273× the warming impact of CO₂ per kilogram (IPCC AR6); F-gases reach several thousand times. Each kilogram captured delivers exponentially more CO₂e reduction — reflected directly in compliance reports.

Result: High-value CO₂e impact per unit — targeting the most damaging, not the most abundant.

04

Grid and Infrastructure-Free Deployment

Deploys wherever emissions peak — no power infrastructure required, no site restrictions. High-emission corridors, underserved zones, and remote facility perimeters are all accessible without grid connection or civil works. All it needs is sunlight.

Result: Wherever sunlight reaches and air pollution peaks — no barriers to deployment.

Where Conventional Approaches Fall Short.

Most urban climate technology focuses on CO₂ — the lowest-GWP greenhouse gas. GreenAiriva's primary targets are nitrous oxide (N₂O, GWP ≈ 273×) and fluorinated gases (GWP up to several thousand times CO₂). Same unit, dramatically more CO₂e per kilogram captured. No competitor names — three category gaps instead.

Centralised Industrial DACCS

  • Requires dedicated industrial sites — geographically remote from emission sources
  • Stores captured CO₂ underground — adding geological risk and long-term liability
  • Energy-intensive: often consumes more energy than the climate value it delivers
  • Cannot operate at street level or within urban environments
GreenAiriva deploys at street level where emissions occur, powers itself with solar, and stores nothing underground.

Grid-Dependent Urban Systems

  • Limited to locations with accessible grid infrastructure
  • Add energy load — contradict the sustainability narrative
  • Scalability constrained by power access and permitting
  • Cannot reach the highest-emission corridors where infrastructure is weakest
GreenAiriva is energy-positive and grid-free — deploys where emissions peak, not where the grid permits.

A Sector With Almost No Infrastructure

  • 95%+ of global DACCS and carbon market investment targets CO₂ exclusively
  • N₂O and F-gas capture infrastructure at urban scale virtually does not exist
  • The highest-climate-value gases are systematically underserved by existing technology
  • Most solutions are solving for the lowest-impact gas while ignoring the worst offenders
GreenAiriva captures gases with 273× to 23,000× more CO₂e per kilogram — operating in a segment where almost no one else is active.

Verifiability

Not an Estimate.
A Documented Result.

GreenAiriva does not report projected or modelled reductions. Every unit produces mass-based data — figures your compliance team, auditor, and regulator can review, challenge, and act on. Regulators are drawing a clear line between models and auditable evidence. GreenAiriva is built for the right side of that line, from the first day of deployment.
