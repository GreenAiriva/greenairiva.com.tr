Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

GreenAiriva ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at greenairiva.com.tr. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

Website analytics: We collect anonymised usage data (page views, session duration, referrer) to understand how visitors use our site. No personally identifying information is collected through analytics.

Contact and enquiry forms: When you submit a contact or demo request form, we collect the information you provide (name, email address, organisation, message). This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry.

GreenAiriva units: Deployed GreenAiriva units collect atmospheric measurement data only. No personal data is collected by unit sensors.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your contact, partnership, or demo enquiries
  • To improve our website and communications
  • To fulfil contractual obligations with institutional partners

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis (KVKK & GDPR)

We process personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — where you have explicitly provided information through a form
  • Legitimate interest — responding to business enquiries
  • Contractual necessity — where a PoC or partnership agreement exists

This policy is aligned with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK, Law No. 6698) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@greenairiva.com.tr.

6. Data Retention

Contact enquiry data is retained for the duration necessary to respond and for follow-up, and deleted upon request. We do not retain data beyond operational necessity.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: info@greenairiva.com.tr
