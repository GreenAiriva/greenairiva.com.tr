Who We Are

GreenAiriva works to improve urban air quality, protect public health, and make concrete, measurable contributions to the fight against climate change. We set a new standard in sustainable urbanism with data-driven, auditable technology.

Good Health & Well-Being

Actively removing greenhouse gases and pollutants from urban airflows — protecting public health where exposure is highest.

Sustainable Cities & Communities

Modular, solar-powered infrastructure that integrates into any urban environment — no grid dependency, no visual disruption, no civil works.

Climate Action

Each unit delivers a documented, auditable greenhouse gas reduction — verified by mass-based measurement, not modelled estimates.

Vision

Our vision at GreenAiriva is to take concrete and measurable steps toward healthier, more livable, and sustainable cities — pioneering environmental accountability at the point of emission, not offset away from it. With our technology aligned with SDG-3, SDG-11, and SDG-13, we reduce greenhouse gas concentrations and protect public health in the environments where people actually live and work. Our ambition is to set a new global standard: active, local, verifiable urban climate intervention — as a documented asset, not a claim.

Mission

Our mission at GreenAiriva is to improve urban air quality and protect public health by offering innovative, science-based solutions that actively capture greenhouse gases from the environments where emissions peak.

We are committed to supporting sustainable urban transformation through advanced technology, real-time data, and institutional collaboration. By prioritising measurable, documented environmental outcomes, we empower cities and organisations to satisfy climate obligations, enhance quality of life, and demonstrate credible progress to the stakeholders that matter.

Through continuous innovation, transparency, and verifiable evidence, we aim to become the trusted infrastructure partner for urban decarbonisation — globally scalable, locally accountable.

Why GreenAiriva?

  • Deployable Urban Scale: Goes beyond centralised, large-scale capture facilities — modular and deployable in any urban environment without grid dependency or infrastructure complexity.
  • Auditable Measurement: Mass-based, real-time data — not modelled estimates. Every reduction figure is structured for third-party verification and compliance reporting.
  • Compliance Value: Supports ESG disclosure, CBAM documentation, and municipal climate plan reporting — delivering measurable climate value that is documented, attributed, and usable.
  • Global Alignment: Designed to satisfy Net Zero 2053, EU Green Deal, CBAM, and TSRS-ESG requirements — scalable from Turkey to any urban market.

Social and Environmental Contribution

GreenAiriva operates year-round with its energy supplied by solar power — an energy-positive system with no grid dependency and no operating carbon footprint. Units are designed for long-service urban deployment: compact, weather-resistant, and low-maintenance.

In terms of documented climate impact per unit of urban footprint occupied, GreenAiriva delivers active, mass-based greenhouse gas reduction at a location-specificity that passive or offset-based approaches cannot replicate. The result is infrastructure that contributes to public health, meets institutional compliance needs, and produces the kind of evidence that auditors, city councils, and ESG teams can act on.
