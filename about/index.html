Vision

At GreenAiriva, our vision is to lead the way in making cities healthier, more livable, and sustainable by taking concrete and measurable actions for environmental health and climate change mitigation. Our system directly contributes to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, particularly SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-Being), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities), and SDG 13 (Climate Action). Through our technology that reduces air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions, we focus on delivering tangible results that protect public health and make cities more resilient to the climate crisis—moving beyond abstract promises to real impact. Our ambition is not only to purify the air in cities, but also to become one of the world’s first projects to operate with a zero-carbon footprint in both operations and production. We aim to be a pioneer in this field and set a new benchmark for sustainability—not just in Turkey, but globally.

Mission

Our mission at GreenAiriva is to improve urban air quality and safeguard public health by delivering innovative, science-driven solutions that effectively capture harmful particles and greenhouse gases. We are committed to supporting sustainable urban transformation by integrating advanced technology, data, and community engagement. By prioritizing measurable environmental outcomes, we aim to empower cities and organizations to meet climate goals, enhance the quality of life for all residents, and foster a culture of sustainability. Through continuous innovation, transparency, and collaboration, we strive to set new standards in clean air technology and become a trusted partner for sustainable progress worldwide.

Why GreenAiriva?

  • Yenilikçi Teknoloji: Doğrudan karbon yakalama (Direct Air Capture – DAC) yöntemlerinin ötesine geçerek, düşük enerji tüketimli, fiziksel adsorpsiyon bazlı bir çözüm sunuyoruz.
  • Gerçek Zamanlı İzleme: Anlık hava kalitesi analitiğiyle akıllı şehir entegrasyonuna olanak sağlıyoruz.
  • Ekonomik ve Çevreci: Karbon kredisi oluşturma, sürdürülebilirlik raporlaması ve yakalanan kirleticilerin ticarileştirilmesiyle müşterilerimize ekonomik değer yaratıyoruz.
  • Küresel Vizyon: AB Yeşil Mutabakatı ve 2050 Net Sıfır Emisyon hedefleriyle tam uyumlu; Türkiye’den dünyaya ölçeklenebilir.

Sosyal ve Çevresel Katkı

GreenAiriva, Birleşmiş Milletler Sürdürülebilir Kalkınma Amaçları’ndan özellikle SKA-3 (Sağlık ve Kaliteli Yaşam), SKA-11 (Sürdürülebilir Şehirler ve Topluluklar), SKA-13 (İklim Eylemi) için doğrudan katkı sağlar. Projemizle, hava kirliliğine bağlı hastalıkların azaltılması, yaşanabilir şehirlerin yaygınlaştırılması ve iklim krizine karşı etkili mücadele mümkün olur.
