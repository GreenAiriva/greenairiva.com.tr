The Overlooked Reality

The Climate Industry Is Almost Entirely Focused on CO₂. N₂O and F-Gases Are Being Ignored.

Global DAC investments, carbon markets, and urban climate technology are overwhelmingly concentrated on CO₂ — the lowest-GWP greenhouse gas. Meanwhile, nitrous oxide (N₂O) and fluorinated compounds — which carry 273× to several-thousand-times the warming impact of CO₂ — receive a fraction of the attention and almost none of the dedicated capture infrastructure.

This gap is precisely where GreenAiriva operates. At street level. Selectively. With every kilogram documented.
