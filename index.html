Our Solutions

At GreenAiriva, we deliver innovative solutions for healthier cities and a cleaner atmosphere. Our system actively captures harmful pollutants and greenhouse gases, contributing to sustainable urban living and global climate goals. By supporting clean air, resilient communities, and environmental responsibility, GreenAiriva helps cities meet critical Sustainable Development Goals.

Good Health & Well Being

We reduce air pollution and improve urban air quality, protecting peopleâ€™s health and enhancing quality of life in cities. Each GreenAiriva unit captures up to 360 kg of particulate matter (PM) directly from the atmosphere every year.

Sustainable Cities & Communities

We empower cities with clean air and data-driven insights, supporting sustainable urban development and livable communities. GreenAiriva collects high-resolution, hourly air quality data from its location, enabling real-time monitoring of atmospheric pollution.

Climate Action

Our system captures harmful greenhouse gases, contributing to global efforts to combat climate change and reduce carbon emissions. Each GreenAiriva unit is capable of harvesting a minimum of 5 tons and up to 30 tons of CO₂e/year greenhouse gases directly from the atmosphere in-situ.
