About GreenAiriva

GreenAiriva is an R&D-driven project aiming to revolutionize urban air purification. Currently in the research and design stage, we are preparing for prototype manufacturing and pilot installations.

  • Stage: Research & Design Completed
  • Next Step: Prototype manufacturing
  • Energy Source: Primarily solar-powered
  • Goal: Improve urban air quality & reduce CO₂, NO₂

Our Future Solutions

Designed to actively capture harmful pollutants and greenhouse gases, our upcoming technology aims to support healthier cities and sustainable climate action.

Healthier Cities

Reduce exposure to harmful air pollutants, protecting public health and enhancing quality of life.

Sustainable Communities

Integrate with smart city data systems for real-time monitoring and proactive environmental management.

Climate Action

Support global climate targets by removing greenhouse gases from urban atmospheres.
