How GreenAiriva works

Each unit draws approximately 600 m³/hour of air from traffic‑dense corridors. Inside, selective, differential media separate target gases—N₂O, CH₄, halogenated/ozone‑depleting species—while a pre‑filter captures particulates down to ~0.2 nm. Filters are easy to regenerate and designed for 30+ years of service life. Outlet gas concentrations are measured and logged to a secure database; data is processed and mapped to support environmental status tracking and MRV. The entire system operates off‑grid using its own solar power.

  1. Intake – High‑throughput fan pulls ~600 m³/h from roadside air columns.
  2. Selective capture – Adsorbents separate N₂O/CH₄ and other target gases; particulates are pre‑filtered.
  3. Regeneration – Media is regenerated on a smart schedule for longevity & efficiency.
  4. Sensing & MRV – Outlet concentrations measured; data logged, processed, and mapped.
  5. Power – Fully solar‑powered; no grid connection required.
