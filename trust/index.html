Data & Security

What We Collect, What We Don't, and Who Controls It.

GreenAiriva units collect environmental measurement data — atmospheric conditions and reduction metrics. No personal data is collected in unit operation. All operational data belongs to the deployment partner.

Data processing agreements are provided as part of every PoC contract. KVKK and GDPR alignment documentation is available for institutional review during procurement evaluation.

Collected
  • Atmospheric measurement readings
  • Unit performance metrics
  • Location-tagged reduction data
  • Operational status logs
Not Collected
  • No personal or biometric data
  • No surveillance or imaging
  • No user tracking
  • No third-party data sharing without consent
Data Ownership

All operational and MRV data generated by a deployed unit is owned by the deployment partner — not GreenAiriva. Data processing agreements formalise this before deployment begins.
