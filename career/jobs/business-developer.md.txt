## Position: Business Development & Funding Manager

### Job Description
GreenAiriva is an environmental technology startup developing innovative, solar-powered urban air purification systems to improve city air quality and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.  
Our project is a high-tech R&D initiative aiming to secure support from TÜBİTAK and international funding programs.  
As a Business Development & Funding Manager, you will engage with local governments, industrial zones, and investors to establish partnerships and manage grant and investment applications.

### Key Responsibilities
- Conducting business development activities with municipalities, industrial zones, and corporate clients
- Preparing applications for domestic and international funding programs (TÜBİTAK, Horizon Europe, EIC, etc.)
- Preparing investor presentations and business plans
- Securing letters of intent and partnership agreements
- Performing market research and competitive analysis
- Contributing to sales and revenue model strategies
- Representing the project at events, exhibitions, and meetings

### Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Economics, Engineering, or related fields
- Experience in project management and/or business development (grant/investment knowledge is a plus)
- Strong presentation and reporting skills with analytical thinking ability
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills; proficiency in English is preferred
- Familiarity with the startup ecosystem and R&D projects is an advantage
- Ability to attend project activities in Ankara when required

### Opportunities & Benefits
- Be part of the founding team of an innovative environmental technology startup
- Active role in TÜBİTAK, EU, and technopark-supported projects
- Social security and tax benefits if technopark application is approved
- Project-based compensation (payment upon grant/funding approval)
- Long-term equity opportunities (equity/sweet equity model)
- Flexible work arrangements (remote/hybrid)

### Application Process
Applications should be submitted via GreenAiriva’s application form.  
Candidates must attach their CV and, if available, links to projects/portfolios.  
No application deadline — evaluations will be conducted on a rolling basis.
