## Position: Software & Data Engineer

### Job Description
GreenAiriva is an environmental technology startup developing innovative, solar-powered urban air purification systems to improve city air quality and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.  
Our project is a high-tech R&D initiative aiming to secure support from TÜBİTAK and international funding programs.  
As a Software & Data Engineer, you will handle sensor data processing, storage, visualization, and integration with smart city platforms.

### Key Responsibilities
- Collecting, processing, and storing IoT device data
- Developing real-time data analysis and visualization dashboards
- Creating APIs and integrating data into smart city systems
- Implementing breakthrough curve analysis and predictive maintenance algorithms
- Ensuring data security and optimizing system performance
- Collaborating with hardware and electronics teams for integration

### Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Data Science, or related fields
- Experience in Python, JavaScript, or similar languages
- Knowledge of IoT data protocols and database management (SQL/NoSQL)
- API development experience (REST/GraphQL)
- Familiarity with data analytics libraries (Pandas, NumPy, etc.) and visualization tools (Grafana, Plotly, etc.)
- Preferably, knowledge of machine learning and predictive modeling
- Ability to attend project activities in Ankara when required

### Opportunities & Benefits
- Be part of the founding team of an innovative environmental technology startup
- Active role in TÜBİTAK, EU, and technopark-supported R&D projects
- Social security and tax benefits if technopark application is approved
- Project-based compensation (payment upon grant/funding approval)
- Long-term equity opportunities (equity/sweet equity model)
- Flexible work arrangements (remote/hybrid) and project-based equipment support

### Application Process
Applications should be submitted via GreenAiriva’s application form.  
Candidates must attach their CV and, if available, links to projects/portfolios.  
No application deadline — evaluations will be conducted on a rolling basis.
